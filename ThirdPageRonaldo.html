Ronaldo's Best Goals


Cristiano Ronaldo has scored hundreds of insane goals throughout his senior career. Although, there are some that stick out more than others. Below is a list of the most insane and beautiful goals he has scored in his career.


Double Kick Against Juventus


Real Madrid and Juventus faced off on April 3, 2018 in the Champions League round of 16. Madrid took the lead in the third minute and controlled the game until minute 64. At that point, they started an attack, and after Ronaldo missed his initial attempt, he made up for it by scoring an incredible double kick.

Screamer Against Porto


Cristiano Ronaldo scored one of his most memorable goals in the Manchester United jersey on this day, April 15, back in 2009 as the Portuguese hit a 40-yard screamer against Porto in the Champions League quarter-final.

Peak Ronaldo Destroys Espanyol Defenders


Real Madrid was leading 3-0 against RCD Espanyol when Ronaldo began an attack, dribbled past many defenders, and finished it off with an outrageous goal in minute 45 of the game.
